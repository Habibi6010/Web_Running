The events are grouped into:

Sprints: 100m, 200m, 400m
Middle Distance: 800m, 1500m
Long Distance: 5000m, 10,000m
Hurdles: 110m/100m Hurdles, 400m Hurdles
Steeplechase: 3000m Steeplechase
Relays: 4x100m, 4x400m
Jumps: High Jump (HJ), Pole Vault (PV), Long Jump (LJ), Triple Jump (TJ)
Throws: Shot Put (SP), Discus (DT), Hammer (HT), Javelin (JT)
Multi-events: Heptathlon (Hep for women), Decathlon (Dec for men)
